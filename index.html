Stop holding the phone.
Wear the compute.

Modern phones are amazing computers with terrible ergonomics. The Compute Brick system keeps the phone on your waist (or docked), then uses ergonomic peripherals for input and AR glasses for display. The result is calmer posture, less thumb strain, and a modular path to “everywhere computing.”
